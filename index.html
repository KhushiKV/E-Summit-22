Get Funded



Get Funded is a day-long startup-investor meet in which start-ups and entrepreneurs from different domains pitch their business model in front of Investors Panel to seek funding.

Startup-Expo



Platform for innovative startups to showcase their product and services and to get in touch with thousands of potential customers and investors.

Panel Discussion


An interactive session which observes exchange of knowledge and experiences, giving a panel of experts and audience a chance to discuss a particular topic.

Lectures



Discover, learn, develop skills that every entrepreneur requires in his journey. A chance to learn from the best experts in the country.

Engendea is a business model competition in which college students team up and compete with each other and present their business plans to the jury. The most impressive ideas are rewarded.

Crazy Ideas


A brainstorming competition targeting high school students to promote both creative thinking and strategic planning bringing out the hidden entrepreneur in them and help them think how they could implement that in real life.
Learn More

Biz Quiz

Ever thought of solving global problems? Here's your chance to contibute feasible solutions to utmost important issues of the society like pollition, traffic, sustainable energy.

Stock Market



Do you want to learn how to make money in the stock market, but do not know where to start? Do you passionately want to become the next 'Warren Buffett'? Learn all about the Art of Stock trading in this workshop.

Digital Marketing


Digital marketing includes all marketing efforts that uses internet. Businesses contest on digital channels such as search engines, social media, mobile apps, email, and their websites to connect with current and prospective customers.

Big Data



Big data analytics helps organizations harness their data and use it to identify new opportunities. That, in turn, leads to smarter business moves, more efficient operations, higher profits, and happier customers.

AI & ML



Modern day technologies like Artifical Intelligence(AI) and Machine learning(ML) have proven to be an indespensable part of new industry standards as they can help make products and services smarter and more valuable.
