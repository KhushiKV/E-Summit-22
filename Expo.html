Startup Expo


About


Startup Expo is a three day startup showcase fest during the E-Summit IIT Indore. It is ideal for startups and companies wanting to market their product and/or grabbing the attention of investors or strategic partners. It is a platform for innovative startups to showcase their product and services and to get in touch with thousands of potential customers and investors. Startups from various sectors / industries are welcome to participate.




Perks



INCUBATION / ACCELERATION OPPORTUNITY

INTERACTION WITH INVESTORS, MENTORS

OPPORTUNITY TO HIRE INTERNS / FULL TIME WORKERS

DIRECT EXPOSURE TO 1500+ IIT INDORE STUDENTS


What do you get?


● 4 sqm stall space to showcase your startup/product.

● Three days full access to E-Summit & Startup Expo 2019.

● Easy visibility to global investors, potential partners and mentors (One-on-one interaction).

● Access to incubators/accelerators.

● Listing on startup expo booklet and E-Summit booklet.

● Logo with link to company's site on E-Summit website.

● Logo on Startup Expo banners and standees.

● Social Media posts mentioning Company's social media handles.

● Branding at different strategic locations during the summit.


Other facilities at the stall:


● 1 Table & 2 Chairs.

● Startup name and logo on the top side of kiosk.

● Electricity Board.

● You can place your creatives / standees in the booth

Registration Procedure


● Last date to register: July 31, 2019

● After reviewing and selection process, selected startups will be notified through emails and calls.

● After selection, startups are required to send a confirmation mail to E-Summit IIT Indore at esummit@iiti.ac.in to confirm their participation. (Last date: 05/08/2019)

● A unique stall no. will be allotted to all the selected startups, along with a point of contact from E-Summit team.

● Startups must pay the fee (Fee: INR 10k only) to E-Summit IITI on or before 15th August 2019. The information will be conveyed through mail.


NOTE: All the dates mentioned above are tentative and may change which will then be notified through emails and calls.
